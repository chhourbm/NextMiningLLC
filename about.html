Our Vision

Convenience. That is the primary goal of our company. Fulfilling all your needs by providing the convenience for you to visit one site, such as Next Mining, as opposed to visiting multiple manufacturer sites. Our mission statement at Next Mining is to be the number one on-line store to fill all your crypto mining hardware needs. As our brand grows, we strive to include and collaborate with any and every ASIC/GPU manufacturers in the cryptocurrency space.

We, at Next Mining, want everyone who purchases products from our company to be happy and satisfied with their purchase. Next Mining wants to offer security in a new industry that is sometimes susceptible to disappointing results. When you order from us you can take comfort in knowing you will receive your order.

Our target demographic are customers both veterans and newcomers to the game of Cryptocurrency. Bitcoin and Cryptocurrency alike have seen a massive bull run in the beginning of 2017 and 2018 is looking to be even bigger than the last.
