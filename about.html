Our Vision

With the rise of Bitcoin’s notoriety in 2017, it catapulted the entire cryptocurrency market into mainstream media. Thus, it created a buzz and demand for Bitcoin and its cryptocurrency counterparts. The next evolution of currency as we know it will arrive sooner than one might think. In order to create the supply to sustain its demand there is a smaller community who are dedicated to mining Bitcoin and various other cryptocurrencies. Fast forward to March 2018, Next Mining LLC is born. We strive to create a one-stop shop and ensure convenience for our everyday consumer who are in need of supplies to support their coin mining.
